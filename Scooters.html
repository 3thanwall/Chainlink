Our Scooters & Bikes

Founded in 2021, Chainlink was built on the idea of transforming urban mobility through a seamless scan-and-ride experience. By placing bikes in city centers, Chainlink aimed to make short-distance travel more convenient, eco-friendly, and enjoyable. From busy commuters to tourists exploring the streets, users could effortlessly unlock a bike with just a quick scan, reducing the hassle of traffic and parking while promoting sustainable transportation. In 2022, Chainlink expanded its fleet by introducing electric scooters alongside its reliable bikes, offering riders even more flexibility. The addition of scooters quickly became popular among those looking for a fun, fast, and practical way to navigate city streets. Today, Chainlink continues to grow as an essential part of urban life, helping communities connect while reducing their carbon footprint. Whether riding for work, leisure, or errands, Chainlink makes it easy to move freely through the city with confidence and style.
